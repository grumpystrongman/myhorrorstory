# Domain: project_management

## Overview
The `project_management` domain centralizes documentation for campaign workflows, sprint cycles, and retrospectives. It defines the operational framework for managing horror story progression while excluding specific technical implementations and content-level details.

## Campaign Lifecycle & Structure
Documentation within the `campaigns/` sub-domain establishes a standardized 28-day (4-week) progression model for horror campaigns.

*   **Standard Campaign Structure (28-Day Model):**
    *   **Phase 1 (Week 1):** Intake and validation of the initial clue chain.
    *   **Phase 2 (Week 2):** Mapping of contradictions and inconsistencies within the narrative.
    *   **Phase 3 (Week 3):** Management of direct contact and escalation of tension.
    *   **Phase 4 (Week 4):** Resolution of the endgame and protection of story continuity.
*   **Operational Flow:** Campaigns follow a linear progression: `Intake` → `Contradictions` → `Escalation` → `Endgame`.
