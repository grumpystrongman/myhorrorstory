Defines the progression impact of the story hint system across three tiers: **Approach**, **Thinking**, and **Solve**.

*   **Hint Impact:** Higher-tier hints (Solve) trigger aggressive state changes, including a **2-day advance** and a **danger increase of 5-6** (plus multipliers).
