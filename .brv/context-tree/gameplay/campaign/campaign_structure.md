## Narrative
### Structure
The campaign is structured as a 4-week (28-day) arc with specific narrative and mechanical focuses for each phase.

### Highlights
Week 1: Intake/Baseline. Week 2: Contradictions/Pressure Testing. Week 3: Escalation/Direct Contact. Week 4: Endgame/Closing Loop.

## Facts
- **Campaign Week 1**: Week 1 of the campaign focuses on intake: establishing baseline evidence and verifying first contact.
- **Campaign Week 2**: Week 2 of the campaign focuses on contradictions: mapping inconsistencies and pressure testing witness accounts.
- **Campaign Week 3**: Week 3 of the campaign focuses on escalation: live interventions and antagonist direct contact.
- **Campaign Week 4**: Week 4 of the campaign focuses on endgame: resolving final branch and closing evidence loop.
