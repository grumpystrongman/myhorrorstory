# Midnight Lockbox - 28 Day ARG Campaign

- Story ID: `midnight-lockbox`
- Duration: 28 days
- Hook: A self-storage unit with no renter sends voice memos predicting what your team will do next.
- Tone: GROUNDED
- Subgenre: supernatural mystery

## Weekly Structure
- Week 1 (Discovery & Curiosity) days 1-7: Validate the first anomaly, establish trust baselines, and seed investigative obsession.
- Week 2 (Escalation & Unease) days 8-14: Push contradictions, introduce manipulative pressure, and degrade certainty.
- Week 3 (Danger & Direct Involvement) days 15-21: Force personal risk, urgent tradeoffs, and irreversible consequences.
- Week 4 (Resolution or Psychological Break) days 22-28: Collapse competing truths into final judgement, compromise, or breakdown.

## Thread Map
- Primary Investigation [main]: An impossible voicemail starts the case at the storage facility.
- Peripheral Incident [side]: Daylight leaks force trust decisions under public pressure.
- Suppressed Truth [hidden_truth]: Inside-job extortion ring using predictive surveillance
- Manufactured Lead [false_lead]: Digital audit-first versus witness-first opening
- Psychological Pressure Arc [psychological]: A final tunnel run decides truth, compromise, or collapse.
