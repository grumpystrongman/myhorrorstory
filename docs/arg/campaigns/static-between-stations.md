# Static Between Stations - 28 Day ARG Campaign

- Story ID: `static-between-stations`
- Duration: 28 days
- Hook: A dead rail line broadcasts private confessions in the players' own voices.
- Tone: CINEMATIC
- Subgenre: psychological horror

## Weekly Structure
- Week 1 (Discovery & Curiosity) days 1-7: Validate the first anomaly, establish trust baselines, and seed investigative obsession.
- Week 2 (Escalation & Unease) days 8-14: Push contradictions, introduce manipulative pressure, and degrade certainty.
- Week 3 (Danger & Direct Involvement) days 15-21: Force personal risk, urgent tradeoffs, and irreversible consequences.
- Week 4 (Resolution or Psychological Break) days 22-28: Collapse competing truths into final judgement, compromise, or breakdown.

## Thread Map
- Primary Investigation [main]: The case opens with impossible evidence and uncertain allies.
- Peripheral Incident [side]: Villain contact escalates while ally trust becomes volatile.
- Suppressed Truth [hidden_truth]: Inside dispatch accomplice
- Manufactured Lead [false_lead]: Telecom trace vs witness-first opening
- Psychological Pressure Arc [psychological]: The final branch depends on ethics, timing, and trust.
