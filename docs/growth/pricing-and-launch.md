# Pricing, Packaging, and Launch Plan

## Suggested Pricing Tiers
- Free: tutorial + teaser case + limited party seats.
- Standard ($9.99/mo): full case library access, party mode, cloud saves.
- Premium ($19.99/mo): director-cut branches, advanced narrator voices, premium media packs.
- Lifetime case pass (one-time): curated story bundle unlock.

## Packaging
- Individual premium story packs for seasonal drops.
- Family/friend party bundles for multi-seat discounts.
- Creator partnership bundles with influencer referral codes.

## Launch Plan
- Closed beta with invited mystery communities.
- Creator preview week with referral tracking links.
- Public launch with free teaser and subscription conversion funnel.

## Retention Loops
- Weekly clue drops and timed challenge events.
- Monthly new story framework release cadence.
- Loyalty unlocks for streaks and referral milestones.

## KPI Framework
- Activation: account created to case start rate.
- Engagement: average sessions per user/week and completion rate.
- Monetization: ARPU, trial-to-paid conversion, churn.
- Referral: invited-to-activated conversion and revenue share.

## Support SLA Concept
- P1 outage: response under 30 minutes.
- P2 critical bug: response under 2 hours.
- P3 functional issue: response under 1 business day.
- P4 general requests: response under 2 business days.
